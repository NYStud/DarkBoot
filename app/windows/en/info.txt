This application enables the black boot screen on unsupported Macs.[return]
	• Confirmed working on OS X 10.9 (Mavericks) to 10.11 (El Capitan)[return]
	• Dark Boot may not work on all Macs use with caution[return]
How to use:[return]
	• Select your desired Boot Color and press Apply[return]
	• Enter your admin password and press OK[return]
	• Reboot twice for changes to take effect[return][return]
How to restore if you can't boot:[return]
	• Boot into your recovery partition (boot holding Command + R)[return]
	• Open Terminal[return]
	• Navigate to the System folder of your boot partition[return]
		cd /Volumes/<boot partition>/System/Library/CoreServices[return]
	• Replace the new efi with the backup[return]
		mv ./boot.efi ./boot.efi.bak[return]
		mv ./boot_stock.efi ./boot.efi[return]
		chmod 644 ./boot.efi[return]
		chown root:wheel ./boot.efi[return]
		chflags uchg ./boot.efi[return]
